Guide to Ann Arbor Restaurants

Welcome to the Restaurant-focused Foodie Guide!


Here we documented some of the most famous restaurants in Ann Arbor based on 3 different cuisine types!


All the restaurants have a small bio and images for you to quickly find them on the streets :)


Kang's Korean Restaurant


Kang's Korean Restaurant is a classic homestyle Korean restuarant, offering Korean food made from over 30 years of experience. Some of their signature dishes include hot stone bibimbap and kalbi.


Kang's is located at the heart of Ann Arbor on South University Street, and you can view their menu below:

Sadako Japanese Restaurant


Sadako Japanese Restaurant is a casual restaurant, offering Japanese dishes from sushi to bento boxes. They are known for high quality foods for low prices.


Sadako is also located at the heart of Ann Arbor on South University Street, and you can view their menu below:

Lan City Noodles


Lan City Noodles is a casual Chinese restaurant, offering authentic Chinese dishes from noodles to rices. Within Ann Arbor, Lan City is one of the most authentic Chinese restaurants.


Sadako is also located at the heart of Ann Arbor on South University Street, and you can view their homepage below:

Condado Tacos


Condado Tacos is a casual Mexican restaurant, offering Mexican street-style dishes from tacos to bowls. Within the city of Ann Arbor, Condado Tacos provides well-known fusion Mexican fare.


Condado Taco Ann Arbor is located on East Liberty Street, and you can view their menu below:

Chapala Mexican


Chapala Mexican Restuarant is a laid-back Mexican locale, offering authentic Mexican food items like enchiladas. With its unique Mexican beachside interiors and exteriors, Chapala brings Mexican flares to AA.


Chapala is located on Main street, and you can view their menu below:

Isalita, Ann Arbor


Isalita is a Mexican locale restuarant with modern rustic feels, offering street-style Mexican food like tacos. With afforadable food and drinks, Isalita is another great Mexican restuarant in AA with modern flares.


Chapala is located on East Libery street, and you can view their menu below:

Mani Osteria and Bar


Mani Osteria and Bar is an up-scale Italian eatery with modern rustic feels, offering street-style Mexican food like tacos and enchiladas. Located in the center of Ann Arbor's food district, Mani is always bustling.


Mani is also located on East Libery street, and you can view their menu below:

Palio Restaurant


Palio Restaurant is an authentic Tuscan fine dining restaurant, offering country-side Italian dishes. With delicious food and drinks, Palio is a great Italian restuarant with an awesome atmosphere.


Palio is located on South Main street, and you can view their menu below:

Casa Dominick's


Casa Dominick's is a laid-back Italian restaurant with traditional Italian eats & pizza, offering ranges of food and drinks. With an unique exterior interior, Dom's is a fun Italian eatery with an awesome vibe.


Dom's is located on Monroe street, and you can view their menu below:

The Earle Restuarant


The Earle Restaurant is a Cozy underground spot for classic French-Italian fare, an extensive wine list & regular live jazz. Located in downtown AA, it is near a lot local attractions, bars, and clubs.
